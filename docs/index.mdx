## The mental model

Rue discovers `test_*.py` modules, filters them down to Rue-marked tests, and then calls those tests.
- Deterministic components? Hardcode values.
- Slightly non-deterministic behavior? Parametrize with multiple inputs.
- Probabilistic contracts? Wrap values into list[Case] and collect metrics.

Rue collects results of `assert` expressions into test results and metrics. 
- CI gate: "Is this safe to ship?" → look at the test suite pass/fail.
- Model swap: "Is GPT-5.4 better than Sonnet 4.6 for my agent?" → look at the metrics.
- Debugging: "Why is accuracy at 61%?" → look at individual test results.

Resources are fixtures. Register SUT factories with `@rue.resource.sut` and other dependencies with `@rue.resource` so Rue can inject and swap them cleanly between runs. No global state. No magic.

Let's go!

`uv add rue`
